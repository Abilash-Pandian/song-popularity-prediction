Ratify

Ratify is a web app where you can find the popularity of your favorite songs. You can use the features such as song duration, acousticness, danceability, energy, instrumentalness, key, liveness, loudness, audio mode, speechiness, tempo, time signature, and audio valence. To access these features, you can visit https://developer.spotify.com/. Enter the values for these features and click "Predict Popularity" to get the predicted popularity of your favorite song.
